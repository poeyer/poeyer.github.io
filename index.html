Telenet Yelo Play AngularJS

The client

Telenet is the leading cable service provider in Belgium. Telenet specialises in the supply of broadband internet, fixed and mobile telephony services and cable television to customers throughout Flanders and Brussels over a powerful HFC (hybrid fibre-coax) network.

The project

The new Telenet Yelo Play web application will be revolutionary. An enhanced and interactive TV guide, watching a live show, record your favorite programs, order a movie & swipe it to your TV or tablet, replay a show from 7 days ago, ... The possibilities are almost limitless! As an application developer I was responsible for the development of the platform.

Hans Anders AngularJS

The client

Hans Anders is a leading optician in the Netherlands, and the second biggest in Belgium. With the Hans Anders app they demo their products to the customers in store.

The project

With the Hans Anders “Brillen”-app, the customer has the opportunity to cycle through all the different options and features glasses can have. Going from multifocal glasses to night glasses, the web application can show them all. As the lead developer I was responsible for the technical analysis and the development of the platform.

Unibind Print Drupal / Symfony

The client

Unibind provides their clients with the most revolutionary document presentation systems. Thanks to over 30 years of research, development and worldwide client feedback, they completed a range of systems and accessories for your printed presentation needs.

The project

The people of Unibind asked me to create a platform where customers could design their own covers for the presentation products Unibind has to offer. The user is guided along 4 steps to personalize their product using the integrated CHILI Publish software. As the lead developer I was responsible for the technical analysis and the development of the platform.
